Genesis

Maria Lorenza Veltri

Founder & CEO

Founded in May 2019 in the United States of America with the ambition to become a unique type of niche digital consulting agency, today, under the inspiration of the eclectic city of New York, Veltmon is an international collective network of passionate minds with hands-on experience and competencies at the intersection between digital marketing, business modeling strategy and performance analysis.

In the last decade, the internet and the social media platforms have become a complex and fragmented overload of information, what we have been learning is that brands and individuals with vibrant online communities are perceived more culturally credible to consumers who, nowadays, don't want to buy a brand, they want to join it.

In this noisy speed changing environment, only who meet the moment will survive.

Our mission is to give storytellers a clear, unique, contextualized perspective to help them to meet the moment.

We collect, measure, analyze relevant information and transform it into actionable insights.

We help brands be the moment.
